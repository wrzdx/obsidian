## Problem
Designing effective resistance training programs from scratch is complex, particularly for beginners who lack sufficient knowledge and experience. At the same time, research shows that users prefer personalized solutions that reduce effort and decision-making complexity, leading to higher satisfaction and engagement[^1].

Despite this, many individuals still train without structured or adaptive programs, even though structured training has been shown to produce significantly better results than unstructured approaches[^2].


## Solution

To address this problem, this project proposes an AI-powered adaptive training system that generates personalized workout programs based on user characteristics and goals.

The system is designed to simplify the process of training planning by automatically selecting exercises, adjusting intensity, and organizing workout structure according to user needs.

## Role of AI

The role of AI in this system is to dynamically adapt training programs based on user input and feedback. The system takes into account factors such as user experience level, available equipment, time constraints, and fatigue levels.

Based on this data, the AI adjusts key training variables such as volume, intensity, and exercise selection to ensure optimal progression and personalization.

[^1]: [The Influence of Personalization on Consumer Satisfaction: Trends and Challenges](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/383006376_The_Influence_of_Personalization_on_Consumer_Satisfaction_Trends_and_Challenges) 
[^2]: [Comparison of structured and unstructured physical activity training on predicted VO2max and heart rate variability in adolescents - a randomized control trial](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/28350537/)
