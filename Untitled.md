<div class="problem-statement"><div class="header"><div class="title">A. Smart Home system</div><div><center> <span class="tex-font-style-bf"><span>Smart Home System</span></span> </center><p><span class="tex-font-style-bf">Description:</span></p><p>The smart home system is designed to manage various smart devices, including Lights, Cameras, and Heaters. Each device has capabilities for being activated or deactivated, alongside particular features and settings that can be manipulated when the devices are in the ON state. Here is the UML class diagram that represents the structure of the code: </p><center> <img class="tex-graphics" src="https://espresso.codeforces.com/c7d4768f2974c3a26ab19e109e6f7341213c9d52.png" style="max-width: 100.0%;max-height: 100.0%;">   <span class="tex-font-size-small">UML</span> </center> <ul> <li> <span class="tex-font-style-bf">Device States:</span> <ul> <li> <span class="tex-font-style-bf">Lights:</span> Each light can be in one of two states: ON or OFF. When the lights are ON, their brightness and color can be modified. When they are OFF, these attributes cannot be changed. </li><li> <span class="tex-font-style-bf">Cameras:</span> Each camera can also be in one of two states: ON or OFF. When ON, cameras can record, and their angles can be adjusted. When OFF, cameras cannot record, and their angles cannot be changed. </li><li> <span class="tex-font-style-bf">Heaters:</span> Each heater has two states: ON or OFF. When ON, the temperature can be set within the specified range. When OFF, the temperature cannot be modified. </li></ul> </li><li> <span class="tex-font-style-bf">Initial Status of the Home:</span> <ul> <li> Lights: There are four lights, all of which are initially turned ON. They have a LOW brightness setting and are configured to emit a YELLOW color. They are not charging. </li><li> Cameras: The system includes two cameras, both initially turned ON. They are not recording, not charging, and are positioned with a camera angle of 45°. </li><li> Heaters: The system has four heaters, all turned ON and set to a temperature of 20°C. </li></ul> </li></ul><p><span class="tex-font-style-bf">Rules:</span></p><ul> <li> When any device is turned OFF, adjustments to any attributes are restricted except the charging status attribute for Lights and Cameras. </li><li> The lights can only be set to either YELLOW or WHITE colors. </li><li> Brightness levels can be categorized as LOW, MEDIUM, or HIGH. </li><li> Each camera's angle can be adjusted independently within a vertical integer range from -60° to +60°. </li><li> The temperature settings for heaters must remain within the integer range from 15°C to 30°C. </li><li> The Lights and Cameras are chargeable, while the Heaters are not. </li></ul><p><span class="tex-font-style-bf">Initial Device IDs and Names:</span> </p><ul> <li> Lights: The lights are identified by device IDs ranging from 0 to 3. </li><li> Cameras: The cameras are identified by device IDs 4 and 5. </li><li> Heaters: The heaters are identified by device IDs ranging from 6 to 9. </li><li> Names: The name of any device should be from the following set of names: {Camera, Heater, Light}. </li></ul><p><span class="tex-font-style-bf">Commands:</span> </p><table class="tex-tabular bordertable"><tbody><tr><td
